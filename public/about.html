Hello! It's nice to meet you.

My name is Cristina Defoe. I'm from Bay Area, California and recently moved to Colorado. I have an 18 year old daughter who just finished her freshman year in college at UC Boulder, majoring in engineering and business and a 12 year old son who is an avid golf and video game player.

While living in Silicon Valley, I worked as a Sales Development Manager at Philips Lumileds. I received a much needed break after moving to Colorado and spent the last year pursuing my other passion interior design. I love the creative process, ability to express myself artistically and technical aspect when it comes to decorating a house.

I hope that my site will inspire you to decorate your space and wish for you a life surrounded with beauty, peace and happiness.

I leave you with a quote that inspires me to dream big, think outside the box and live life to the fullest.
