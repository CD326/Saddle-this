Hello! It's nice to meet you.

My name is Cristina Defoe. I'm from the Bay Area and recently moved to Colorado. I have an 18-year-old daughter who just finished her freshman year in college at CU Boulder while majoring in engineering, and a 13 year old son who is an avid golf and video game player.

While living in Silicon Valley, I worked as a Sales Development Manager at Philips Lumileds. I received a much-needed break after moving to Colorado and spent the last year pursuing a hobby of mine which is interior design. I love the creative process, the ability to express myself artistically, and the technical aspect which comes with decorating a house.

I hope that my site will inspire you to decorate and create a space surrounded by beauty, peace, and happiness.

I leave you with a quote that inspires me to dream big, think outside the box, and live life to the fullest.
