Contemporary Mountain House with Rustic Appeal and Modern Aesthetics

This is a blog site about interior design, focusing on Contemporary Rustic Mountain House with a touch eclectic vibe. After putting different styles together, there's a sense of harmony and balance in the midst of imperfections. This is my story based on personal experience after buying, decorating and designing our home.

Architecture

There is an art to the structure and science in the design of a custom home. This home is designed to accomodate my family's active lifestyle where we can find beauty and peace in our surroundings even with our busy schedules.

Home Decor

This home is designed by finding special furnitures, arts or collections that makes it feel authentic. It is mixed with different styles, colors, textures and artwork that invokes meaning and personality.

Outdoor Space

The exterior space is designed to bring nature into the home and is an extension of the surrounding architecture and natural beauty of the environment. Being so close to nature, means we may get occasional visits from deers, bears and mountain lions.
