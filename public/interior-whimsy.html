My Thoughts

Design Challenge

Before my family and I moved to Colorado, we had a collection of Asian and African arts throughout our old house in California. One of the challenges I ran into was - can I still use the artworks we already have and still maintain that rustic mountain look? Trying to figure out where to place our old collections and artworks was harder than I thought. I wanted that glossy magazine look, but it didn't feel right. So, I made changes and designed it based on how we live and what we love. That worked! Now, we have a home that combines rustic, modern, and eclectic collections. Not to mention a touch of balance and harmony. Happy designing!
