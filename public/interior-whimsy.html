My Thoughts

Design Challenge

Before moving to Colorado, we had Asian and African arts in our home in California. It made sense then the way our house was designed - there was a good flow. The challenge is can we place these same artworks in our new home since it has a mountain rustic feel? Trying to figure out where to place our new and old furnitures, collections and artworks was harder than we thought. We wanted that coveted glossy magazine look, but it didn't feel right. So, we made changes and designed it based on how we live and what we love. That worked! Now, we have a home that combines rustic, modern and eclectic collections with a touch of balance and harmony. Happy designing!
